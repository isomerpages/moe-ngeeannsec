<p>In Ngee Ann Secondary School, we believe in providing a holistic education
for your child/ward. The school also believes in working closely with parents/guardians
to provide support and guidance to our students.</p>
<p>As such, there will be a parent engagement session for you to learn more
about our school, our Total Curriculum and provisions.</p>
<p>Details about this session will be sent via Parents Gateway.</p>
