<h2>Structure of the Programme</h2>
<p>Technopreneur Programme is designed to cultivate students' entrepreneurial
mindset and technological skills through a 3-year programme.</p>
<p>In the first year, students are introduced to the Design Thinking framework,
which guides them in identifying, understanding, and ideating solutions
to real-world problems. Training includes digital prototyping and communication
skills, and cybersecurity awareness through the Sentinel programme in partnership
with MINDEF, enabling students to effectively develop and present their
ideas.</p>
<p>In the second year, students continue to deepen their knowledge and skills
in coding and cybersecurity, and apply them to their innovative projects.</p>
<p>From the second to third year, they are also provided with opportunities
to participate in competitions, learning journeys, and interactions with
industry professionals, allowing them to collaborate with peers and gain
insights from experts in the field.</p>
<h3><strong>Eligibility Criteria for Technopreneur</strong></h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>All interested students will sit for a selection test at the start of
the term. Shortlisted students will be invited to the programme.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students admitted to the school through Direct School Admission (DSA)
in Coding will be automatically enrolled in the programme.</p>
</li>
</ul>
