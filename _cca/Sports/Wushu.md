### About Us

**VISION**: What does the CCA want to achieve?&nbsp;

Every wushu student a champion (values, area of achievement).  

  

**MISSION**: What does the CCA set out to do?

Nurture and develop athletes with character.  

  

**LEARNING OUTCOMES**: By the time the students leave the CCA, what characteristics do they exhibit?

(1) Students to excel in wushu.&nbsp;  

(2) Students to exhibit strong school values (integrity and resilience).&nbsp;  

(3) Students will be lifelong learners for wushu.
