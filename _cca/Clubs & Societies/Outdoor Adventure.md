<h3>About Us</h3>
<p><strong>VISION:</strong> What does the CCA want to achieve? ODAC people
are fit and adventurous.</p>
<p><strong>MISSION</strong>: What does the CCA set out to do? To instil in
young people a love for an active lifestyle.</p>
<p><strong>LEARNING OUTCOMES</strong>: By the time the students leave the
CCA, what characteristics do they exhibit?</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students learn skills that would allow them to appreciate and enhance
their experience in the outdoors.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students develop leadership skills that allow them to be competent and
confident decision makers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students will persevere and be resilient in the face of challenges and
obstacles they face in their lives.</p>
</li>
</ol>
