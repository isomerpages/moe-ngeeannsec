<h4><strong>Principal’s Message 2026</strong></h4>
<p>At Ngee Ann Secondary School, or NAS, we nurture <strong>Value Creators of the Community</strong> —
NASians who care deeply and make a positive difference to themselves and
others. More importantly, we want every student who joins us to feel that
they belong, are valued, and have the confidence to grow.</p>
<p>Our school was founded by The Ngee Ann Kongsi in 1994 with a mission to
give back to the community through education. This spirit continues to
shape who we are today. We intentionally develop NASians to be <strong>Thinkers, Collaborators and Communicators</strong>  <strong>with a Growth and Abundance Mindset</strong>.
Our students are also <strong>digitally confident and AI-literate</strong>,
using technology meaningfully to learn, create and contribute.</p>
<p>At NAS, every student is given opportunities to discover their interests
and grow their strengths. Through our <strong>Skills Discovery Electives,</strong> students
explore areas such as AI, robotics, STEM, sports, music and the arts. Our <strong>NAS EdVentures</strong> and
authentic learning experiences allow students to apply their learning to
real-world challenges and community needs.</p>
<p>Students who wish to pursue their passions further can do so through programmes
such as <strong>G3 Computing, Higher Mother Tongue, the Enhanced Art Programme</strong>,
and our school-based <strong>Talent Development Programmes</strong> across
Science &amp; Mathematics, Humanities &amp; English, Technology &amp; Coding
and Art. We also broaden our students’ perspectives through partnerships
with organisations such as the Singapore University of Technology and Design,
Singapore University of Social Sciences, Temasek Polytechnic and the Science
Centre, as well as overseas learning experiences in places like China and
Japan.</p>
<p>At the heart of NAS is a <strong>strong Culture of Care and Growth</strong>.
Every student is known, cared for, and encouraged to step out of their
comfort zone. We believe that every NASian can succeed, and we journey
closely with each student so that no one grows alone.</p>
<p>Our teachers and staff model this commitment by continually strengthening
their professional practice, earning recognitions such as the Lower Secondary
Science Teachers’ Showcase 2026, the Computing Teacher Awards 2024 and
2025, Caring Teacher Awards and the President’s Award for Teachers. When
our educators continue to grow and flourish, we believe our students benefit
too.</p>
<p>As a school, we are deeply grateful to our School Management Committee,
parents, alumni and partners. Your trust, encouragement and support form
the strong foundation that has enabled NAS to grow from strength to strength
over the years.</p>
<p>To our NASians — and to our new students and your families — my hope for
you is simple and heartfelt.</p>
<p>May you grow into confident young people who are willing to try, willing
to learn and willing to persevere when challenges come your way. May you
discover your strengths and have the courage to keep growing, even when
the road ahead is not easy. Most importantly, may you use your talents,
passions and skills to make a positive and meaningful difference to yourself
and to others.</p>
<p>At Ngee Ann, we stand behind you as <strong>One Team, One NAS, One Family</strong>.
This is your school, your community and always, <em>your Home to Grow In</em>.
<br>
<br><strong>Go chase your rainbow, NASians!</strong>
<br>
<br>Ms Poh Shi Hui
<br>Principal, Ngee Ann Secondary School</p>
